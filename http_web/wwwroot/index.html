Meet Bitcoin

The innovative payment network and a new kind of money.

more charts..

Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer technology that operates with no central authority or banks; managing transactions and the issuing of bitcoins is carried out collectively by the network. Bitcoin is open-source; its design is public, nobody owns or controls Bitcoin and everyone can take part. Through many of its unique properties, Bitcoin allows exciting uses that could not be covered by any previous payment system.
